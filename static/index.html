FAQ

Questions answered.

How is this different from Notion or Evernote?
Those are generic note-taking tools. Coviction is purpose-built for the VC workflow: automatic entity extraction, thesis tracking, conviction scores, knowledge graphs, and AI briefs that understand the difference between a company, a metric, and a sector trend.
What AI models power the analysis?
We use frontier LLMs (GPT-4o, Claude) for entity extraction, brief synthesis, and deal memo generation. Your notes are processed in an encrypted environment and never used for model training.
Can I use this during live meetings?
Yes. The capture interface is designed for speed — title, body, sector tag, done in 10 seconds. Voice memos let you dictate between conversations. Image capture snaps slides and auto-transcribes.
How does the conviction score work?
Every mention of a company or thesis is a signal. Positive signals compound the score; silence causes gradual decay (30-day half-life). The result is a living metric showing how actively you're tracking something, with a full audit trail.
Is my data private?
Completely. Each user's data is isolated. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share observation content with other users or use it for model training. Your investment intelligence is yours alone.
